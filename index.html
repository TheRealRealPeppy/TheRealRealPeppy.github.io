About Me

I am a cybersecurity engineer specialising in the offensive field. I also happen to do mods in my spare time, my drive to be an artist is to create things I like and can actually share with people & friends.

I've started drawing in August 2025 thanks to a very good friend of mine that inspired me, I've met countless inspiring people since and continue to do so. So I can only be very greatful to her for that push that genuinely improved how I spend the time I have.
